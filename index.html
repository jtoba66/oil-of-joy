Our Ministry

Oil of Joy Music is more than a band – we are a ministry dedicated to creating sacred moments through music. Based in Calgary, we serve the surrounding areas with a heart for worship and a commitment to excellence.

Our experienced musicians and vocalists bring together diverse talents to create an atmosphere of reverence, joy, and spiritual connection. Whether you're planning an intimate home gathering or a grand celebration, we tailor our ministry to honor your special moment.
